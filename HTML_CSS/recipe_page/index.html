Sheet Pan Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken & Veggies

This recipe is a lifesaver on busy nights! It's incredibly simple to prepare, requires only one pan for easy cleanup, and delivers juicy chicken and perfectly roasted vegetables infused with lemon and herbs. Serves 4-6.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (approximately 1.8 - 2.3 kg)
  • 1 large lemon, halved
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried thyme (approximately 5ml)
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary (approximately 5ml)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder (approximately 2.5ml)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 500g baby potatoes, quartered
  • 500g broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat & Prep: Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a large sheet pan with parchment paper or foil.
  2. Season Chicken: Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Rub it all over with olive oil, thyme, rosemary, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Squeeze the juice from one lemon half into the cavity of the chicken. Place the other lemon half inside the cavity.
  3. Arrange Vegetables: Spread the quartered potatoes, broccoli florets, chopped bell pepper, and sliced onion evenly across the sheet pan. Drizzle with a little more olive oil (approximately 15ml) and season lightly with salt and pepper.
  4. Place Chicken on Veggies: Place the seasoned chicken directly on top of the vegetables.
  5. Roast: Roast for 60-75 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through (internal temperature reaches 74°C/165°F) and the vegetables are tender and slightly browned.
  6. Rest & Serve: Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving and serving with the roasted vegetables.
